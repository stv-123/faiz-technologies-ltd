About Us

FAIZ TECHNOLOGIES was registered on 19th July 2022 Business under the REGISTRATION OF BUSINESS NAMES ACT (cap, 449 section 14) and later incorporated as a limited company on 24th February 2025 and a leading national safety solution provider. From risk assessment to delivery of work, in partnership with government, business and other organizations we provide integrated solutions that meet the safety challenges of the 21st century.

Founded in 2022, we have quickly risen to become an industry team player by insisting on the highest training and safety standards. Those standards coupled with our integrity, originality and personality means you get a personal and tailored safety service and, most importantly, the best service for you.

We are proud of our distinct cultures and strong ethical values that guide how we conduct our business and develop positive relationships with our customers, investors and communities we work with.
